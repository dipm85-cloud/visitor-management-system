Deferred polish items captured during OH-015. These are intentionally outside the current milestone because OH-015 is limited to navigation, shell, and account access polish without business logic or schema changes.

- Full People UX redesign, including denser list scanning, clearer status filters, and improved create/edit flow.

## Account And Shell

- Advanced account profile menu, including profile details, preference management, and richer account metadata.
- Deeper responsive table behaviour for narrow desktop and mobile widths, especially for People, Visitors, and Reference Data lists.

## Workspace Flow

- More complete workspace breadcrumbs once cross-app navigation patterns settle.
- Expanded dashboard quick actions and status widgets once related capabilities and data contracts are stable.

## Visitors Coexistence

- The native Visitors workspace foundation is active with today, planned, signed-in, walk-in and overdue summaries.
- Legacy VMS is retained as a temporary internal application until planned visits, walk-ins, staff sign-in/sign-out, history, reports, configuration, agreements, GDPR, exports and printing are migrated and regression-tested.
- Replace each “Open Legacy VMS” handoff incrementally; do not remove the legacy destination until kiosk and staff workflow parity is confirmed.
